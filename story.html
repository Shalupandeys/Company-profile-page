STARTUP

Dev Creations and Solutions was founded by Mr. Devraj, a visionary who turned personal challenges into opportunities. As the president of the Mars Forum Committee at REVA University, he faced hurdles in designing and printing for college events, inspiring him to master the process. His passion for solving real-world problems led to the creation of a startup dedicated to providing high-quality, affordable design and merchandise solutions. Today, the company thrives on its core values of creativity, grit, and client satisfaction. Dev Creations and Solutions continues to empower others with innovative services rooted in its founder's journey.
